InstaPOS

As a final project in RU, me and three other students did an app for Valitor, payment solutions company.

The app would work as a point-of-sale device and take payments through NFC or by manually inputing the card information.

The app was developed with Xamarin, written in C#, and the API was in .Net.

I touched on almost all areas: I worked on the API, the business logic on the client and created most of the UI.
